Haze 101 is dedicated to two areas: Haze Science Background, and the TERC VHS-1 Sun Photometer Manual which can assist users in building their own inexpensive sun photometer. They may later wish to contribute data in Haze-SPAN's Data Collection area of this web site. Contributing data is not limited to the TERC VHS-1 model. See the Frequently Asked Questions in Forrest's corner for further information about haze and instruments.
